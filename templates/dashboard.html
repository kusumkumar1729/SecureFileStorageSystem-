Encryption Technologies

SecureFile leverages a combination of proven encryption algorithms to provide top-tier security for your files. Learn more about the technologies powering our platform.

AES

Advanced Encryption Standard (AES) is a symmetric-key algorithm trusted by governments and organizations worldwide for its strength and efficiency. SecureFile uses AES-256 for maximum protection.

RSA

RSA is an asymmetric encryption method ideal for secure key exchange. It ensures that only authorized parties can decrypt your files, even over unsecured networks.

Blowfish

Blowfish is a fast, symmetric-key cipher designed for high-speed encryption. SecureFile employs Blowfish for scenarios requiring rapid processing without compromising security.

Hybrid

Our hybrid approach combines symmetric and asymmetric encryption for the best of both worlds: speed and security. This ensures efficient file encryption and secure key distribution.
